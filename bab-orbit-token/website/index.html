What is BAB Orbit?

BAB Orbit (ORBT) is a utility token issued by BAB INDUSTRIES (Pty) Ltd, South Africa. It is designed to reward loyal customers and partners across all BAB businesses — from catering and auto-detailing to legal solutions and solar energy.

Redeem ORBT for exclusive discounts across the BAB ecosystem.

Partner Benefits

Use ORBT at partner brands joining the BAB rewards network.

Token Utility

ORBT works across every BAB business from day one.

  • Earn ORBT on every purchase at BAB Catering, BAB Auto Detailing, BAB Solar Energy, BAB Logistics, and more
  • Redeem for service credits, priority bookings, and bundled offers
  • Stake ORBT to hold a membership tier with ongoing perks
  • Pay for selected digital products and online courses with ORBT
  • Partner businesses can list campaigns and promotions paid in ORBT

ORBT is a utility token only. It does not represent equity, debt, or any right to dividends or profit sharing.

How to Get ORBT

01

Install MetaMask

Download the MetaMask browser extension from metamask.io. Create your wallet and store your seed phrase safely offline.

02

Add Polygon Network

In MetaMask, add Polygon Mainnet. Network ID is 137, RPC is https://polygon-rpc.com.

03

Import ORBT Token

In MetaMask, click Import Token and paste the ORBT contract address (published here after launch).

04

Earn or Buy

Earn ORBT by transacting with BAB businesses, or participate in launch campaigns when announced.
