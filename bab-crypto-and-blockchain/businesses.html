BUSINESS ROADMAP

What businesses should we focus on?

Start with businesses that generate recurring revenue, solve clear market pain points, and are easier to scale regionally with compliance controls in place.

Priority Tier 1 (Launch First)

  • Stablecoin Payment Gateway: API and checkout tools for merchants and online stores.
  • Cross-Border Remittance App: low-cost settlements between key African corridors.
  • Crypto OTC Desk: high-volume buy/sell execution for corporates and HNIs.

Priority Tier 2 (Scale Next)

  • Institutional Custody Service: secure storage and governance for enterprise clients.
  • Tokenized Asset Marketplace: fractional ownership products for property and commodity projects.
  • Compliance-as-a-Service: KYC/AML monitoring and wallet risk scoring for fintechs.

Priority Tier 3 (Strategic Expansion)

  • Blockchain Academy: paid training for developers, founders, and compliance officers.
  • Startup Incubator: co-build early-stage web3 startups aligned with BAB priorities.
  • Research & Advisory Unit: policy and enterprise advisory for blockchain adoption.
