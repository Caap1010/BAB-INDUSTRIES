ABOUT BAB CIVIL CONSTRUCTION

From Broken to Built Better

BAB Civil Construction was founded on the belief that “Broken Ain’t Bad” – it’s a starting point for building stronger, smarter and more beautiful structures across Africa.

Who We Are

Based in South Africa, BAB Civil Construction delivers end‑to‑end civil and building solutions for residential, commercial and public sector clients. We combine hands‑on site experience with disciplined project management to deliver projects safely, on time and within budget.

Our team understands the realities on the ground – informal settlements, tight budgets, complex sites and ambitious visions. Whether it’s a unique pallet‑built restaurant in Alexandra or a large‑scale housing development, we approach every job with the same commitment to quality and accountability.

Our Mission

To transform broken, under‑utilised and undeveloped spaces into safe, functional and inspiring structures that uplift communities and clients.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s leading civil construction partner for bold ideas – especially projects that others deem too risky, too different or too difficult.

Our Values

Integrity, craftsmanship, innovation, respect for people and efficient use of resources – including alternative methods like pallet‑based and modular construction.
