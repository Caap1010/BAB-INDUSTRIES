About BAB Catering

BAB Catering is the official hospitality, events, and food services division of BAB Industries — one of Africa’s most ambitious multi‑industry ecosystems.

Guided by the corporate philosophy “The Hate U Give”, BAB Catering delivers high‑quality, culturally respectful, scalable catering solutions across corporate events, funerals, private dining, and large‑scale gatherings.

BAB Catering follows the same brand language, visual identity, operational discipline and premium service ethos that defines BAB Industries.
