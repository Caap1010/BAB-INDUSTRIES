About BAB Industries

BAB Industries is an Africa‑born, multi‑industry holding company built on the belief that Broken Ain’t Bad. We identify broken systems, overlooked opportunities and unfair barriers — then design businesses that turn those gaps into long‑term value.

Our portfolio spans finance, technology, property, sustainability, food, media, logistics, sports and education. Each venture is focused, system‑driven and aligned under one founder‑led vision: build the organisations and tools needed for the next generation of Africa.

Our Purpose

  • Identify where people are underserved or locked out.
  • Build scalable brands and platforms that serve those gaps.
  • Combine technology, creativity and discipline in execution.
  • Create wealth, ownership and opportunity across the continent.

Our Identity

Tagline: The Hate U Give

Slogan: “The absence of evidence is not the evidence of absence.”

These principles express our belief that potential exists before proof, and that doubt or hate can be converted into fuel, focus and execution.
