About BAB Consulting Group

Africa-born, founder-led, built on the belief that Broken Ain’t Bad.

BAB Consulting Group is the advisory arm of the BAB ecosystem—created to help businesses transform broken systems into billion-rand opportunities. We work with organizations across industries to design bold strategies, fix critical operations, and unlock digital transformation.

Our philosophy is simple: the problems everyone avoids are the ones hiding the most value. Where others see failure, we see data. Where others see chaos, we see patterns. We believe the absence of evidence is not the evidence of absence—potential is always there, even if it isn’t yet visible.

From early‑stage founders to established enterprises, we partner deeply with our clients, not as consultants who just write reports, but as operators who help execute and make change real.
